## Findings

### Resolved — undefined terms distracted from the test

`Disposal` and `Available Cash` were initially used but not defined. Both definitions were added to Clause 1.1.

### Resolved — synthetic facility clauses were out of page order

Clause 6.4 originally appeared after Clauses 12.3 and 13.1 in the markdown file. The clauses were reordered by page to avoid unnecessary participant confusion.

### Moderate — the third question may appear answerable from the temporary prohibition

The Waiver Letter says no Distribution may be made through 30 September 2026. A participant may therefore prefer a short “not during the waiver period” response. The pack remains non-definitive because the broader proposed transaction requires classification, facts, consents, proceeds treatment, financial inputs, and legal interpretation.

This is an intended session test: does the fuller caution improve the handoff or feel evasive?

### Moderate — synthetic timing cannot establish production ROI

The corpus is intentionally small and clean. Time saved in these sessions is directional evidence about workflow and format, not a defensible production ROI estimate.

### Minor — “72% maximum” remains an attractive misreading

The pack counters this explicitly. Facilitators should record whether participants still describe 72.0% as the amended covenant threshold.

## Pre-session release decision

The materials are suitable for Phase -1 sessions provided:

- the criteria freeze date and planned decision date are completed first;
- facilitators follow the confidentiality script;
- synthetic timing is not presented as production ROI; and
- all participant corrections are recorded rather than defended away.
