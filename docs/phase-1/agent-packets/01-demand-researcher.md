# Packet 01 — Demand Researcher

## Input context

- `README.md`
- `docs/phase-1/README.md`
- `docs/phase-1/three_question_reviewer_pack.md`
- Phase -1 pass/adapt/stop criteria in `docs/phase-1/decision_record.md`

## Exact task

Design or refine exactly ten non-leading workflow-review questions and a repeatable 45-minute session script that elicit recent behaviour, current workarounds, analyst and reviewer burden, trust effects, adoption blockers, and costly next steps.

## In scope

- Target practitioner/reviewer framing
- Confidentiality-safe questioning
- Baseline-versus-pack comparison
- Serious-session definition
- Evidence capture requirements

## Out of scope

- Product implementation
- Market-size claims
- Sales copy presented as research
- Contacting participants

## Owned files

- `docs/phase-1/outreach_questions.md`
- `docs/phase-1/session_script.md`

## Expected output and acceptance criteria

- Exactly ten questions
- Recent-behaviour questions precede hypothetical preferences
- Script fits 45 minutes
- Baseline pack is hidden until the assisted-review stage
- Costly next step is classified without inflating polite interest
- No prompt requests confidential information

## Completion evidence

- Question count
- Timing allocation total
- Mapping from each pass/adapt/stop signal to at least one question or observation
- List of leading questions removed or rewritten

## Stop and escalate when

- The target participant role cannot be defined
- A valid session would require real confidential documents
- The session cannot distinguish analyst time from reviewer burden

## Do not

- Treat compliments or meeting attendance as demand
- Ask participants to disclose employer, deal, borrower, or lender identities
- Change decision criteria after sessions begin
